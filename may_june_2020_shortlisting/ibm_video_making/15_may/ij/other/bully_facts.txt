1)	More than half of people under 25 have experienced bullying at some point.
2)	People who are bullied, often go on to bully others.
3)	More than a third of people go on to develop Social Anxiety and Depression as a direct result of bullying.
4)	Almost a quarter of those who have been bullied have had suicidal thoughts.
5)	Those who bully are far more likely to have experienced stressful and traumatic situations in recent times.
6)	17% of people have experienced cyberbullying.
